Indiana, U.S.A.

Coatesville

Coatesville, Indiana sits about 30 miles West of Indianapolis, and is about 15-20 minutes from Danville, Indiana.

About

Weather

Indiana weather is all over the place. During the first 3-4 months of the year, it is quite chilly, but fluctuates back and forth. Once May rolls around, it gets much warmer, and stays at a rough estimate between 70-90 degrees up until late October to early November.

Population

Coatesville is home to around 1,000 people. As of 2025, multiple homes were built and created more living space, which grew the population of the town immensely.

Sports

Soccer and Football are a big hit in the town, as well as Baseball.
